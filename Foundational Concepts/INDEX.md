# Foundational Concepts

*Guides that introduce the key ideas behind We Practices.*

Each foundational concept guide is written for felt understanding, not academic knowledge. The main text is accessible to anyone. An optional systems appendix provides the deeper architecture for readers who want it.

## Suggested sequence

1. **Understanding Separation** — What happened. The condition the practices are a response to.
2. **Understanding Shared Reality** — What was lost. How shared reality fragments and why rebuilding it matters.
3. **Understanding Reunion** — What the journey moves toward. Not a destination but a living process.
4. **Understanding the Relational Field** — What you'll be working in. The medium of every practice.
5. **Understanding Our AI Companion** — What it means when relational intelligence crosses the digital threshold. Not a prerequisite for practice; read when curiosity arises.
